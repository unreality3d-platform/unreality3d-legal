1. Your Right to Data Deletion

At Unreality3D, we respect your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. This policy explains what data we delete, what we retain for legal compliance, and how to request deletion.

Simple Deletion Process

We've made data deletion straightforward and transparent. Most requests are processed within 30 days, with immediate account deactivation.

  • Email admin@unreality3d.com with your deletion request
  • We'll verify your identity and process your request
  • You'll receive confirmation when deletion is complete

2. How to Request Data Deletion

Email Request

Send an email to admin@unreality3d.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your Unreality3D username or email address
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account
  • Any specific concerns or questions about the deletion process

Identity Verification

To protect your account security, we will verify your identity before processing deletion requests:

  • Email must be sent from your registered account email address
  • For PayPal-linked accounts, we may require additional verification
  • If you no longer have access to your email, contact us for alternative verification methods

3. Data Deletion Timeline

1

Immediate (Within 24 hours)

Account deactivation and login access removal. Your content becomes inaccessible to other users.

2

Within 7 Days

Personal profile information, content metadata, and platform analytics deleted from active systems.

3

Within 30 Days

Complete deletion from Firebase databases, backup systems, and analytics platforms. GitHub integration disconnected.

4

Confirmation

Email confirmation sent to verify that your data deletion is complete.

4. What Gets Deleted

Personal Information

  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • PayPal integration and authentication tokens
  • Creator profile information and settings
  • Personal contact information and communications

Content and Analytics

  • Content metadata and descriptions
  • Usage analytics and interaction data
  • Quest progress and achievement data
  • Platform navigation and feature usage data

Technical Data

  • GitHub integration settings and repository connections
  • Firebase authentication tokens and sessions
  • Platform configuration and preferences

5. What We Retain

Legal and Financial Compliance

Some data must be retained for legal, financial, or security reasons. This retained data is anonymized where possible and securely stored with restricted access.

Financial Records (PayPal Transactions)

  • Transaction History: Required for tax compliance and financial auditing (7 years)
  • Payout Records: Creator earnings and payout history for tax reporting
  • Anonymization: Personal identifiers removed while maintaining transaction integrity

Security and Legal Requirements

  • Fraud Prevention: Anonymized data to prevent platform abuse
  • Legal Compliance: Records required by law for specific timeframes
  • Platform Security: Technical logs for security incident investigation

6. Your Content and GitHub Repositories

Platform-Hosted Content

  • Content hosted on Unreality3D servers is deleted with your account
  • Published URLs become inaccessible within 24 hours
  • Content analytics and metadata are removed completely

GitHub Repositories

  • Your Repositories: Remain under your full control and ownership
  • Platform Integration: Disconnected but repositories are not deleted
  • Your Choice: You can keep, transfer, or delete repositories independently
  • Template Usage: You may continue using downloaded templates

7. Third-Party Services

PayPal Data

  • We disconnect PayPal integration from your account
  • PayPal retains transaction data according to their policies
  • Contact PayPal directly to manage their data retention
  • Revenue sharing stops immediately upon account deletion

GitHub Integration

  • Platform access to your repositories is revoked
  • GitHub data retention follows GitHub's policies
  • Your repositories and GitHub account remain unaffected

Firebase (Google Cloud)

  • User data deleted from Firebase databases
  • Analytics data anonymized or deleted
  • Authentication tokens invalidated

8. Partial Data Deletion Options

You don't have to delete your entire account. We offer selective data removal options:

Content-Only Deletion

  • Remove specific content while keeping your account
  • Delete published projects individually
  • Clear analytics data for specific content

Payment Data Removal

  • Disconnect PayPal integration
  • Switch to free account status
  • Retain creator account without monetization

Integration Disconnection

  • Remove GitHub integration only
  • Disconnect PayPal authentication
  • Clear specific third-party connections

9. Data Export Before Deletion

Before requesting deletion, you can export your data:

  • Content Analytics: Download usage statistics and performance data
  • Creator Information: Export profile and account settings
  • Transaction History: Download payment and earnings records
  • Content Backup: Ensure you have copies of your creative work

Contact admin@unreality3d.com to request a data export before deletion.

10. Consequences of Data Deletion

Permanent Action

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Consider the following before proceeding:

Loss of Access

  • Cannot recover your username or creator profile
  • All published content becomes inaccessible
  • Professional URLs stop working immediately
  • Platform tools and SDK access removed

Financial Impact

  • Pending PayPal payouts may be delayed
  • Future earnings from existing content stop
  • Access to revenue analytics removed
  • Tax reporting data becomes unavailable (except for compliance records)

Community Impact

  • Your content becomes unavailable to users who purchased access
  • Community contributions and interactions are removed
  • Creator profile and achievements deleted

11. Reactivation Policy

Due to our commitment to permanent data deletion:

  • No Reactivation: Deleted accounts cannot be restored
  • New Account: You may create a fresh account with a new username
  • Content Recreation: Previous content and analytics cannot be recovered
  • Username Availability: Your deleted username may become available for others

12. Children's Data

For users under 18:

  • Parent or guardian can request deletion on behalf of minors
  • Expedited processing for users under 13
  • Additional verification may be required
  • Educational account data handled according to applicable privacy laws

13. International Compliance

This policy complies with international privacy regulations including:

  • GDPR (European Union): Right to erasure and data portability
  • CCPA (California): Right to delete personal information
  • COPPA (United States): Parental rights for children's data
  • Global Standards: Industry best practices for data deletion

14. Technical Deletion Details

Database Deletion

  • Primary records removed from Firebase production databases
  • Development environment data cleared
  • Backup systems purged according to retention schedules

Analytics Platforms

  • User-specific analytics data deleted or anonymized
  • Platform-wide statistics may retain anonymized usage patterns
  • No personal identifiers retained in aggregate data

Content Delivery

  • Content removed from all hosting platforms
  • Professional URLs deactivated
  • Cache systems cleared globally
