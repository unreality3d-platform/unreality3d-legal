1. Introduction

Unreality3D LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Unreality3D platform, a Unity 6+ WebGL content creation and publishing platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • PayPal Login: When you authenticate with PayPal, we receive your name, email address, and PayPal account verification status.
  • Creator Profiles: Username, public display information, and content metadata.
  • Payment Information: Transaction history and payout preferences (processed securely through PayPal).

Technical Information

  • GitHub Integration: Repository names and deployment information for content publishing
  • Firebase Analytics: Platform usage, content interaction, and performance metrics
  • Unity WebGL Data: Application usage metrics and performance analytics
  • Content Analytics: View counts, interaction data, and user engagement metrics

Usage Information

  • Content interaction data and analytics
  • Platform navigation and feature usage
  • Quest completion and interactive system usage
  • Publishing and deployment history

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Platform Services: Providing authentication, content publishing, and creator tools
  • Content Publishing: GitHub repository management and automated deployment
  • Payments: Processing content sales and creator payouts through PayPal (95% creator payout)
  • Communication: Platform updates, creator support, and service notifications
  • Analytics: Improving platform performance and user experience
  • Creator Support: Providing technical assistance and platform optimization

Data Processing Locations

Your data is processed and stored using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) infrastructure, primarily in United States data centers. GitHub integration data is processed through GitHub's global infrastructure.

4. Information Sharing

We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • PayPal Integration: Payment processing and payout services (95% creator revenue share)
  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google Cloud) for platform infrastructure and GitHub for content publishing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Creator Choice: Public content and profiles as configured by creators
  • Analytics Partners: Anonymized usage data for platform improvement

5. PayPal Integration

Our platform integrates with PayPal for authentication and payments. When you use PayPal services through our platform:

  • PayPal's Privacy Policy applies to data they collect
  • We receive limited account information necessary for platform functionality
  • Payment processing is handled directly by PayPal's secure systems
  • 95% of revenue goes directly to creators, 5% supports platform operations
  • You can manage PayPal data sharing preferences in your PayPal account

6. GitHub Integration

For content publishing and deployment:

  • We access repository information necessary for automated publishing
  • GitHub's Privacy Policy applies to data they collect
  • Repository names and deployment history are stored for platform functionality
  • You maintain full ownership and control of your GitHub repositories

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Firebase security rules and authentication
  • PayPal's PCI-compliant payment processing
  • OAuth 2.0 compliant authorization with professional callback URLs
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Environment-aware deployment protecting creator data

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Export your created content and analytics data
  • Disconnect PayPal or GitHub integrations
  • Control your content visibility and monetization settings

9. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for platform services or as required by law:

  • Account Data: Deleted within 30 days of account closure
  • Transaction Records: Retained for financial compliance requirements
  • Content Analytics: Anonymized after account deletion
  • GitHub Integration: Repository access removed immediately upon disconnection

10. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. International Users

Our services are provided from the United States using Firebase (Google Cloud) and GitHub infrastructure. By using our platform, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. We will notify users of material changes through the platform or email. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
